Mission Briefing

How to Play

LOCKDOWN is a top-down shooter set inside a crumbling apartment complex overrun by the undead. You made the call to hole up and wait it out — but supplies are gone, the groaning outside your door is getting louder, and the horde isn't just growing. It's organizing. Gear up, scavenge every floor, and shoot your way to the exit before whatever is leading them finds you first.

01 🏚️
Explore the Building

Push through dark hallways, flooded basements, and barricaded stairwells. Your apartment complex is massive — and most of it hasn't been touched since Day One. Every room could hold answers, or something worse.

02 🎒
Scavenge for Supplies

Ammo. Food. Weapons. Anything left behind by people who didn't make it. Raid abandoned units, check every cabinet and crate — running dry in the middle of a horde is not a mistake you get to make twice.

03 🧟
Avoid the Infected

They were your neighbors. Now they're a numbers problem. Slip past when you can, shoot when you have to. Every bullet you waste is one less you'll have when things get worse — and they will get worse.

04 🧠
Something Has Evolved

The horde doesn't wander anymore. It waits. It herds. Something deep in the building has changed — and it knows you're trying to leave. Find the exit before it finds you.
