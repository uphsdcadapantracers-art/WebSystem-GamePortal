Mission Briefing

How to Play

DEAD ZONE is a top-down escape game where you must navigate through a zombie-infested facility. Solve environmental puzzles, scavenge for supplies, avoid or eliminate threats, and find the emergency exit before you're overwhelmed. One wrong move could be your last.

01
🗺️
Navigate the Facility

Move through dark corridors and locked rooms. The facility layout is randomized — no two runs are the same. Study your surroundings carefully.

02
🧩
Solve the Puzzles

Doors won't open without a reason. Find clues, activate terminals, and solve the mysteries left behind before the outbreak consumed everything.

03
🧟
Avoid the Horde

Zombies react to sound and movement. Stay quiet, stay hidden, or find weapons to fight back — but ammunition is scarce. Choose wisely.

04
🚪
Find the Exit

The emergency exit is sealed. Gather the keycards, restore the power, and make your run for it before the horde breaks through the final barricade.
